AI Solutions is thrilled to announce the expansion of our operations into Mandalay and Naypyidaw. This growth allows us to better serve our clients across Myanmar and support the increasing demand for innovative AI solutions in business automation, data analytics, and cloud services.

  • Naypyidaw Office: Central hub for government partnerships and large-scale AI deployments.

Impact

This expansion not only strengthens our presence in Myanmar but also allows local businesses to access cutting-edge AI technologies and consultation services more efficiently. Our teams will focus on accelerating digital transformation initiatives and fostering innovation in various industries.
